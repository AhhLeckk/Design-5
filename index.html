Our Philosophy

Built on love,
guided by the child

K–6
All Grade Levels

Our mission is to provide an atmosphere of learning that is LOVING emotionally, and physically and spiritually SAFE — one that creates a living and unique Montessori environment where children learn through "doing."

We instill tolerance, thoughtfulness, consideration, and responsibility. We celebrate differences in others. The focus is on the heart of the child, while studying their strengths and passions. IMA values diversity and seeks families of all racial, cultural, religious, and economic backgrounds.

International Montessori Academy was founded by Inayat Unissa Inayat-Khan Bergum, whose grandfather Hazrat Inayat Khan personally met with Dr. Maria Montessori. IMA carries a legacy of uniting children through Love, Harmony, and Beauty.

❤️
Love
Children are born with the natural tendency to love — the platform from which they learn most effectively.
🕊️
Harmony
When there is understanding and knowledge, harmony is reached. We rise above differences together.
🌸
Beauty
We inspire children to have ideals, appreciate beauty, and find joy and wonder in learning.
🧠
Follow the Child
We create independent learners who attain their personal best at their own level and rhythm.

Common Questions

Things families ask us

What are your student-to-teacher ratios?+
Our small class sizes ensure every student receives individualized attention throughout their day — a hallmark of the Montessori approach.
What is the Montessori method?+
Developed in the 1900s by Dr. Maria Montessori — a scientist, mathematician, and physician — the Montessori method is based on the premise that each child is born with a drive to develop themselves. A child learns best through physical activity and the senses, given freedom to discover, explore, and create in an enriched environment with supportive encouragement.
What are Montessori materials and why are they different?+
Montessori materials are self-directed and self-correcting — they activate a desire to learn more because they engage children both physically and mentally. Each material develops one perception at a time and places the responsibility for finding answers on the child, building independence and self-motivation rather than dependency on the teacher.
Who are your teachers?+
All lead teachers hold Montessori credentials and are highly qualified, experienced, innovative, and creative. Our teachers emphasize moral instruction, tolerance, and kindness. They are supported by ECE-qualified teacher's assistants at every campus.
Do you accept families of different backgrounds and faiths?+
Absolutely. International Montessori Academy values diversity and actively seeks families of all racial, cultural, religious, and economic backgrounds. Our founding philosophy of Love, Harmony, and Beauty was built on celebrating differences — not minimizing them.
What extracurricular activities are available?+
Extracurricular activities vary by campus and by season. Please visit your specific campus page on our website, or contact the campus director, to learn which activities and enrichment programs are currently being offered.
How do I find tuition and fees?+
Tuition varies by campus and program level. Please visit our Tuition & Fees page or contact your preferred campus directly. We encourage all interested families to reach out regardless of financial situation — we are committed to making Montessori education accessible.
